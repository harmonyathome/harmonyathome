Meet Our Founder ♡

Ajonay Harris

Welcome to Harmony@Home, a boutique childcare agency founded by Ajonay — a seasoned nanny, dance educator, and mentor with over 10 years of hands-on experience. After a decade of caring for children from newborn through the teen years, Ajonay created Harmony@Home to redefine what quality in-home care truly means: intentional, nurturing, and in rhythm with each family's unique flow.

At Harmony@Home, we believe that childcare is more than supervision — it's connection. Our caregivers are hand-selected for their ability to create structure without rigidity, encourage creativity, and cultivate a calm, joyful environment where children can grow confidently.

From first feedings to first performances, our agency is built to meet families wherever they are — with personalized support, dependable communication, and a deep respect for your home, your children, and your values.

Harmony@Home isn't just a service. It's a partnership built on trust, alignment, and care that feels like family.
